What is AI Playground?

AI Playground is a platform where you can submit your program and compete with others.
You will have fun mindstorming, coding and sharing ideas.

AI Problems

Interesting AI Problems! Basically, you can submit your program and compete with others. Competitions between AI and AI can be really fierce. And there will be a rating calculated after each round.

Traditional Problems

There are also many traditional problems, based on standard input and output, using the rule of ACM-ICPC contest. You can practice your programming skills here. What is special, however, is that you can also add your own problem here.
